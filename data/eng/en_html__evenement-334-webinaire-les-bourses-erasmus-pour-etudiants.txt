Webinar: Erasmus+ scholarships for students

The Erasmus+ 2021-2027 program supports cooperation projects that aim to promote the global excellence and internationalization of higher education establishments.  Two Erasmus+ shares offer student scholarships: students:

- Study stock market: finances the physical and/or hybrid mobility of higher education students, regardless of their sector and cycle of studies.
- Excellence scholarship for an EMJM master: finances individual mobility for students participating in the Erasmus Mundus Master Master (EMJM).

The Erasmus+ Tunisia National Office is organizing a webinar entitled "Erasmus+ opportunities for students" January 15, 2024 at 2:00 p.m.  Objectives: Know the opportunities, understand the participation criteria and procedures, exchange with the former Erasmus+scholarship holders.
